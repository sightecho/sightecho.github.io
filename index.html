Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SightEcho ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the SightEcho mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name and email address when you register.
  • Images: photos you capture or select to submit for analysis. Images are uploaded to our secure servers and processed by AI vision models.
  • Location data: approximate location (country/region) derived from device GPS or IP address to provide geographically relevant context. Precise location is not stored.
  • Device information: device model, OS version, and app version for diagnostic purposes.
  • Purchase information: in-app purchase records processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store payment card details.
  • Usage data: analysis requests, feature interactions, and error logs to improve the service.

2. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera solely to allow you to capture images for analysis. We do not record video, stream camera feed, or access the camera in the background. Camera access is only active when you explicitly initiate a photo capture within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the AI-powered image analysis service.
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts.
  • Process in-app purchases and manage credits.
  • Deliver text-to-speech audio narrations.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Google (Gemini API): images and prompts are sent to Google's Gemini API for AI analysis, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud: our infrastructure provider for hosting, storage, and text-to-speech services.
  • Apple / Google Play: for in-app purchase verification.

5. Data Retention

Your images and analysis results are stored on our servers until you delete them from the App or delete your account. Account data is retained for as long as your account is active and deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@sightecho.app. You may also delete your account and all associated data directly from the App settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, JWT authentication, and Google Cloud security infrastructure to protect your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
