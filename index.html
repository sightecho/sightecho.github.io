What SightEcho does

🎙️

AI Docent

One tap and an expert-style narration explains the place, art, food, or sign in your photo — read aloud, even with your screen off.

🗺️

Trips & Stories

Photos group into trips automatically, and each trip becomes a travel story and a shareable recap card.

💛

Lifelong Memory

Your trips are kept as a living archive. Revisit them a year — or ten years — later.

🌐

Multi-language

Narration and audio in many languages. Read or listen in the language you prefer.
