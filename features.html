Sign mode

Read signs, menus, tickets, and notices in context.

Point SightEcho at text you can't read. It translates the words, then tells you what actually matters for the situation you're in.

  • Street signs, menus, tickets, and notices

  • Warnings, rules, and instructions that affect what to do next
  • Helpful phrases for directions, ordering, or asking for help
