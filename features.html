Scenery mode

Understand the places in front of you.

When you see a plaza, cathedral, bridge, viewpoint, or street scene, take a photo. SightEcho explains what the place is, why it matters, and what to notice.

  • Landmarks, natural scenery, street scenes, and architecture
  • Identify the place before you start searching
  • Learn the history, background, and viewing points
  • Get a quick explanation or a deeper guide-style answer

  • Details to look for while you stand in front of it

Meal mode

Know what the dish is, and how people eat it.

Take a photo of local food or a menu. SightEcho helps you understand the dish name, ingredients, flavor, and how it is usually eaten.

  • Dish names and plain-language explanations
  • Key ingredients, spice level, and allergy clues
  • How locals usually eat or share the dish
  • Helpful context before ordering from a menu

Sign mode

Read signs, menus, tickets, and notices in context.

Point SightEcho at text you do not understand. It translates the meaning and explains what matters for the situation, not just the words.

  • Street signs, menus, tickets, and notices
  • Translation plus the main point in plain language
  • Warnings, rules, and instructions that affect what to do next
  • Helpful phrases for directions, ordering, or asking for help
